Buttons

Buttons are one of the most integral part of a web interface.

Types of Buttons

Particle UI is having a quite a variety of buttons. Some of the pre-defined solid buttons are primary, secondary, error, succcess, dark, info, light, disabled, warning.

Outline Buttons

Particle UI also have outline buttons, like primary, secondary, success, error, warning, info and dark.

Other types of Buttons

There are other types of buttons like Button with a icon, floating action button which are quite useful. Particle UI is also having three different sizes of button icons, small, medium and large.
